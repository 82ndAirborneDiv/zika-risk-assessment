Things to remember:

  • The recommendations featured in this app focus on travel to destinations outside the United States and US territories. Visit the CDC website for more information about Zika in the United States. Special recommendations may apply to people who live in or travel to areas of the US where Zika is being spread by mosquitoes.
  • Your risk of Zika may also be affected by the elevation at your travel destination. Learn more about Zika and elevation, and check our Zika travel notices for more information related to your specific destination.
  • CDC is routinely updating information as we learn more about Zika. Check the CDC Zika website often for the latest guidance and recommendations.
